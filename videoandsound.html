Remix Glitch Video/Sound Proccessing Net Art Final

My concept is about a boy who didn't believe in ghost, so the ghost haunt him. To make subject(me) look like a ghost I use transparency firm on top of the image, so that the human look ghost like and the background is still solid. For the sound I use hearth beat to so that the subject is scare and also I put in small detail sound effect in the video to bring out the store for example typing, clicking, turn off light, door close and other to let the view know what the subject is doing. The filming was done with Canon EOS Rebel T6 and the software I use to edit was Adobe Premiere Pro.
